Bakery

Hi, I'm Shaun Sheeran the owner of Sheeran's Bakery. I got into baking because I always loved watching my mother and grandmother bake. Home baked goods for me have always been a sign of family and love. Whenever I bake whether it be a holiday or a regular weekday it's made with love, and with the goal of bringing families together over something delicious.
